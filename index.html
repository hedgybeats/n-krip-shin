Welcome to N-KRIPT

The ultimate encryption desktop app that keeps your sensitive files away from prying eyes.

With N-KRIPT , you can safeguard your confidential data with just a few clicks, whether you're sharing it over the cloud or storing it on your computer.


Here's how it works:

When you encrypt a file, N-KRIPT generates an encryption key and an initialization vector (IV) and gives them to you.

You MUST copy these values and store them somewhere safe, because you'll need them to decrypt the file later!

By default, N-KRIPT uses the trusted Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) algorithm to encrypt your files.

Key vault:

N-KRIPT also includes a built in key vault. Say goodbye to the hassle of managing multiple encryption keys as you can now store all your keys and initialization vectors under one secure password.

To get started, all you need to do is set a master password that's between 20 and 32 characters long. Once you've done that, you can easily add keys to the vault and view them whenever you need them, without having to remember multiple passwords.

Rest assured that your data is in safe hands with N-KRIPT's vault. Every row of data stored in the vault is encrypted with the AES-256-CBC encryption algorithm, making it virtually impossible for anyone to access your files without the master password.

N-KRIPT is designed with simplicity and security in mind. Whether you're a tech-savvy user or not, you'll find our app incredibly easy to use, yet incredibly powerful. Protect your data with N-KRIPT and experience the peace of mind that comes with knowing your files are safe and secure.
